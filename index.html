Privacy Policy

We, the developers of the Math Blaster game app, are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle any information collected from users of our app.

The Math Blaster game app does not collect any personal information or data from its users. We do not require any permissions to access your device's data or features.

Since no data is collected, we do not use or share any personal information with third parties. The app is designed to operate solely within the device and does not transmit or store any user data externally.

The Math Blaster game app is intended for general audiences, including children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has provided us with personal information, they should contact us immediately, and we will delete that information from our records.
